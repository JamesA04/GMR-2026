Itinerary

Our wedding will be taking place across two venues for the two main parts of the day - the ceremony and the reception. We would love for you to join us for the entire day.

The day will start at 1:30pm at the Assembly House in Norwich City Centre. The day will end at midnight at the Old Rectory in Great Melton.

Departing the venue

Departure from the Old Rectory will take place at midnight. Any accommodation is unlikely to be within safe walking or cycling distance, with no street lights, and there is no public transport that heads towards Norwich.

If not travelling by car or sharing with others, the best option for departure will be taking a pre-booked taxi. Please pre-book these yourselves in advance of the day from a local firm such as ABC, Courtesy or Goldstar. Uber, Bolt, and similar app-based taxi services have not yet reached Norfolk (seriously) and will not be available.



Accommodation

Accommodation options

We are not providing or arranging any accommodation for guests as part of the wedding.

Although we are not recommending any specific accommodation, we suggest booking hotels - either for the night before, or morning of, the wedding (or both) - close to Norwich City Centre.

There may also be suitable holiday lets and AirBnBs in the villages surrounding Great Melton. You may wish to group up with family and friends to stay in one for the night of the wedding.
